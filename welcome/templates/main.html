What is it for?

This is non-profit service for fans of Magic: the Gathering to share and discuss their drafts.

As a newcomer to MtG (my first drafts were in February 2013) for me to have some lessons learned and progress in this game, I often discuss my past drafts with more experienced players. However, it can be a hassle to post your drafted deck together with picks on forums or IM. I realized why not to make a web-service for exactly that purpose.

How does it work?

Your Magic the Gathering online beta client saves your picks during drafts in a text file. You can review these settings under 'Account' -> 'Game History' -> 'Auto Save Draft Logs' All you need to do is to locate such a file and submit it.
