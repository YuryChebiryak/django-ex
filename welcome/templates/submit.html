How does it work?

Your Magic the Gathering online beta client saves your picks during drafts in a text file. You can review these settings under 'Account' -> 'Game History' -> 'Auto Save Draft Logs' All you need to do is to locate such a file and submit it.

After submitting you will be automatically redirected to the page with your draft.
